Dual Preview - Chrome Extension

Start seeing mobile the way users do.

Up to 80% of web traffic is mobile, yet websites are built on desktop. Testing mobile there is a pain. Stop fighting with clunky previews and dev tools. Dual Preview is a free Chrome extension that syncs desktop and mobile views instantly. One screen, zero hassle.

Design, test, and demo without the clunky workflow.

Mobile testing shouldn't mean non-stop window tweaking. Dual Preview gives you a unified workspace to analyze how other sites scale, test your own features in real-time, and present polished responsive designs to clients or stakeholders. One tool for your entire workflow - from initial research to the final Sprint Review.

Everything you need for a faster, smarter mobile workflow.

Side-by-side in one click

Paste any URL and see it on mobile and desktop simultaneously. No DevTools, no tab switching.

Real device viewports

iPhone SE, iPhone 14, Pixel 7, Samsung S23 - precise presets for the phones your users actually own.

Multiple panels

Compare three mobile sizes at once, or run two desktop breakpoints in parallel. Add panels dynamically.

Screenshot both panels

Capture mobile and desktop in one image. Share in Slack, drop into Figma, paste into a ticket - everyone sees both.

QR code to real device

Generate a QR for any panel URL and scan it with your phone. Feel the actual experience in your hand.

Open in PageSpeed

One click from any panel to PageSpeed Insights - check performance of the exact URL you're reviewing.
